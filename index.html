Get Started With Kian Sian

Kian Sian, a major contracting, maintenance & logistic services company, is the outcome of the joining together of two powerful long established construction & logistic services companies with glorious past experiences and performance records on many of Saudi Arabia’s most iconic projects since many years. Kian Sian combines an outstanding reputation for implementation and delivery excellence, developed over the past 15 years in the construction of some of Saudi Arabia’s largest and most challenging major projects, with its reservoir of experience, technical know-how and execution capability.


The mission of Kian Sian is to provide premium contracting and construction management services to International Standards throughout Saudi Arabia in a timely and economical fast track methodology. Kian Sian builds enduring value and trust using a dedicated team approach to continuously improve client/ customer satisfaction, quality and the work environment. Kian Sian, is a recognized leader in Saudi Arabia for the construction and execution of major civil, mechanical and electrical with works and operations with an enviable record for on-schedule completion, quality workmanship, honesty and integrity.
